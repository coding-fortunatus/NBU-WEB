    About

    Learn More About Us

    Welcome to NBU, where tradition meets innovation. Established to provide a world-class education, our institution combines the rich cultural heritage of Nigeria with the esteemed academic standards of British education.

    Our mission is to foster academic excellence and personal growth, preparing future leaders through diverse programs and state-of-the-art facilities, supported by a dedicated faculty.

    At Nigerian British University, we believe in the power of education to transform lives and shape the future. Join us and be part of a community where your potential is realized, your achievements are celebrated, and your future begins.

    Vice - Chancellor

    Prof. Hakeem Fawehinmi

    Welcome to Nigerian British University. As your Vice-Chancellor, it is my privilege to lead this distinguished institution where academic excellence and cultural diversity converge.

    Our commitment is to provide a transformative education that equips our students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in an ever-changing world. At Nigerian British University, we nurture innovation, foster inclusivity, and uphold the highest standards of teaching and research.

    Together with our dedicated faculty and staff, we are here to support you every step of the way as you embark on your educational journey. I look forward to witnessing your growth and success as a member of our vibrant academic community.
